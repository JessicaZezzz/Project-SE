Registration of Fully-Funded Chevening Masters
Scholarship 2022 is Finally Opened! Check This Out!
4 January 2022

Chevening scholarship registration is open again. For undergraduate students who are ready to take Masters or Masters education in the UK, you can take advantage of this registration. Reporting from the chevening.org page, the opening of the Chevening scholarship application for the year 2022/2023 has been open since August 3, 2021. If you apply for this scholarship, you will get various benefits. For example, not only are tuition fees fully funded, students can have access to some of the best universities in the world, an exclusive network, and the opportunity to explore Britain's diverse culture due to extensive networking. In addition, scholarship recipients also receive a round-trip air ticket, and then obtain health insurance by the UK's National Health Service (NHS).

Chevening scholarship funding comes from the UK Government's Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office and partner organisations. Candidates who pass will also get a VISA for education from the British government directly. To apply for this scholarship, all Chevening scholarship applicants must submit educational documents. The scholarship selection process usually takes a minimum of 8 months from the application deadline.

The criteria for candidates to look for in the Chevening scholarship program are those who demonstrate the passion, vision and skills needed to shape a better world. In addition, as alumni, all scholarship recipients are expected to return to Indonesia with knowledge and networks for career development. Then what are the conditions?

All Chevening applicants must submit an education document, references and a UK university offer (Letter of Acceptance) unconditionally. If you are conditionally selected for the Chevening Scholarship, you will need to submit these documents in order to remain in the process. Some of the requirements that need to be met to apply for a Chevening scholarship:
1. Indonesian citizens or Timor Leste.
2. Have a bachelor's degree (S1) or bachelor's degree, because this scholarship is for master's degree studies.
3. GPA at S1 level must be 3.00.
4. Have 2 years experience. This work experience can be part-time and full-time, covering at least 2,800 hours of work, this includes volunteering or internships. Not a campus required internship.
5. English proficiency to the standard required by UK universities, such as IELTS 6.5. iBT TEOFL 79, Pearson PTE Academic 58, or Cambridge English Advance (176 overall score with a minimum score of 162).

The following is the selection schedule:
1. Registration opens: August 3, 2021
2. Registration closing: November 2, 2021
3. File assessment: November-December 2021
4. Call: February 2022 interview
4. Deadline for collection of educational references and documents: February-late April 2022
5. Scholarship candidate interview: 28 February-29 April 2022
6. Announcement: Early June 2022
7. Study period starts: September-October 2022
